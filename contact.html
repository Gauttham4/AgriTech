Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions

How do I register as a farmer on AgriConnect?

To register as a farmer, click on the "Join as Farmer" button on our homepage or navigate to the registration page. Fill out the required information including your contact details, farm location, and the types of produce you grow. Our team will review your application within 48 hours.

What services do you provide for soil testing?

Our soil testing services include comprehensive analysis of soil pH, nutrient content, organic matter, and texture. We provide detailed reports with crop-specific recommendations for fertilizers and amendments. You can request soil testing through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team.

How do I place an order for agricultural products?

To place an order, browse our product catalog, select the items you wish to purchase, and add them to your cart. Proceed to checkout, select your preferred payment method, and confirm your delivery details. You can track your order status through your account dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery for eligible orders. All online transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption to ensure your payment information remains safe.

How accurate are your weather forecasts and crop recommendations?

Our weather forecasts utilize data from multiple meteorological sources and have an accuracy rate of approximately 85-90%. Our crop recommendations are based on soil data, climate conditions, and historical performance metrics. While we strive for maximum accuracy, natural variations can occur, and recommendations should be used as guidelines along with your farming experience.
